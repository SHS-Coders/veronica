  • Challenge #1: DOGE

    My Procedure
    1. Draw a large circle for the head.
    2. Draw a two smaller circles for the eyes.
    3. Draw two even smaller circles for the irises.
    4. Draw two even smaller circles for the pupils.
    5. Draw two triangles for the ears.
    6. Draw a large rectangle for the body.
    7. Draw a smaller rectangle for the neck to connect the head and body.
    8. Draw four thinner rectangles for the legs.
    9. Draw an arc for the tail.
